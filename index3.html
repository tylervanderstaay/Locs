About Drink Assist

Have you ever had friends over for drinks and didn't know what to make with what everyone brought?

It’s easy to become confused or overwhelmed when it comes to making drinks. You are expected to know the different bases, mixers, add-ons and garnishes!

At Drink Assist, we know you’re not a bartender.

We’ve created a platform to help you identify your ingredients and match you with the perfect drink, along with the recipe to go with. You’ll never have to worry about messing up a cocktail (and accidentally making another Frankenstein potion) again.

Our Team

This project was created by Kaylin Flam, Aydene Lopez, Ephrain Reliford, and John Vanderstaay during the University of Central Florida's Coding Bootcamp.

We are learning full-stack software engineering, and this project uses both a cocktail database and YouTube API to filter your ingredients, match recipes and provide users with a YouTube video to go along with it.

This project is apart of our growing portfolio to showcase the critical topics we are learning throughout the bootcamp.
